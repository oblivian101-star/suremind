Our History

Milder Touch Mental Health & Wellness LLC was founded in the midst of the global COVID-19 pandemic—an era that exposed deep gaps in mental health access, support, and continuity of care. Born out of a desire to meet the rising emotional and psychological needs of individuals, families, and communities, Milder Touch emerged as a beacon of hope, healing, and restoration during uncertain times.

From the beginning, our vision has been clear: to deliver evidence-based, holistic care that honors the whole person—mind, body, and spirit. We recognize that mental health is not one-size-fits-all and that true healing begins with compassion, clinical excellence, and culturally sensitive support.
